Electrician

Master the skills of electrical installation, maintenance, and repair

Course Overview

The Electrician program is designed to produce skilled professionals capable of handling complex electrical systems. Students master residential and industrial wiring, fault finding, and energy distribution. Emphasis is placed on safety protocols (NEO/IEC standards) and modern energy-efficient technologies.
